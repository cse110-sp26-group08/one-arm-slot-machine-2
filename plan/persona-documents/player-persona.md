# Player Persona for a user who wants just entertainment

### Name: Joy a Highschool student

### Description:

Joy is a high school student who just discovered slot machines. She and her friends are addicted to slot machines and compete with each other. Joy usually plays between classes, or while on the bus. She does not have a source of income and just wants to enjoy playing the game.

### User Stories:

#### Basic Needs

- [x] As a casual player, I want the game to be free to play, so that I do not have to spend my parents’ money on the game.
- [x] As a casual player, I want the games to be fast paced and not take too much time, so that I can play between classes and on the bus.
- [x] As a casual player, I want the game to be visually engaging with sounds and animations, so that I do not get bored while playing.
- [x] As a high school student, I want to play on my phone, so that I can play anywhere like on the bus.
- [ ] As a free to play player, I want free tokens every day, so that I can keep on playing if I lose everything one day.

#### Additional Features

- [x] As someone who plays with her friends, I want to compare my winnings with my friends’ (like through a leader board or ranking system), so that we could compete and have fun.
- [x] As a casual player, I want there to be multiple slot machines/themes, so that I don't get bored playing the same game again and again.
- [x] As a free to play player, I want prizes within the game with no actual monetary value (customizations), so that I can play/compete fairly.
